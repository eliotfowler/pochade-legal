Privacy Policy
Pochade
Effective date: March 8, 2026 · Last updated: March 8, 2026
The short version: Pochade keeps your data on your device. We do not collect personal information, require accounts, or track you. We use privacy-friendly, anonymized analytics to improve the app.

Who We Are

Pochade is developed by John Fowler ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the Pochade app.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

Pochade is a painting reference tool. All image editing, value studies, cropping, and grid overlays happen entirely on your device. Your reference photos and session data are stored locally and are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party service. The app cannot browse your full photo library.

We do not collect your name, email, or contact information. We do not require accounts or login. We do not access, upload, or share your images. We do not sell data to third parties, and we do not display ads or track you across apps.

Analytics and Diagnostics

We use TelemetryDeck (TelemetryDeck GmbH, Augsburg, Germany) to collect anonymous, aggregated usage analytics and crash diagnostics. This helps us understand how the app is used, fix bugs, and prioritize improvements.

TelemetryDeck anonymizes all identifiers on your device before transmission using a double-hashing technique. Neither we nor TelemetryDeck can trace any signal back to an individual user. No cookies are used, and no data is shared with advertisers or data brokers.

Anonymized data collected may include an untraceable device identifier (hashed on-device), app usage events, device type, OS version, app version, and crash reports.

TelemetryDeck's SDK is open source at github.com/TelemetryDeck. Their full privacy policy is at telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

In-App Purchases

Pochade offers an in-app purchase processed through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information — all transactions are handled by Apple under their privacy policy.

Your Rights

Since Pochade does not collect personal information, the rights typically granted under privacy regulations like GDPR and CCPA are largely not applicable. The anonymized analytics collected by TelemetryDeck cannot be linked back to you. You can delete the app or individual sessions at any time to remove all local data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
